Hello!

I’m Tammy and I’m a Canadian illustrator and Engineering Manager. I like to joke that I need art to balance out the science of my everyday work. My illustrations tend to be colourful and playful. My style is evolving with every project I take on. I’m currently challenging myself with a limited palette and lineless art.

I spent my free time drawing, reading, and playing farming simulator video games. I also like to watch Youtube videos about living in small spaces. I used to be all about tiny houses but they weren’t small enough. I now watch a lot of videos about living in a van or a car. I don’t know where this obsession comes from as I’m a maximalist at heart. I’d like to start a vlog but I’m shy.

If you'd like to work with me, contact me at tammyleedraws@gmail.com.
