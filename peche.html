Pêche

This odd, peach-headed being is a character I’ve been drawing since high school. They started off with the name ‘Peachboy’ but, like so many do, they changed their name to something a bit more sophisticated as they grew older. Pêche is a creative who finds life challenging so has developed a rich inner life filled with imagination and fantastical stories.

Peach goblins are little peaches that run around and have arms and legs and personality for days. If I’m stuck for something to draw I usually draw peach goblins to warm up.
