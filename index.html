I live in Edmonton, Alberta, Canada which is on Treaty 6 Territory as well as Métis homelands and Métis Nation of Alberta Region 4. This is traditionally the land of many First Nations such as the Nehiyaw (Cree), Denesuliné (Dene), Nakota Sioux (Stoney), Anishinaabe (Saulteaux), and Niitsitapi (Blackfoot).
