Stickers

For the last 3 years, I have designed a monthly sticker for my Patreon supporters. That's over 36 unique stickers and yes, I have a shoebox FULL of the extras! While I do have the equipment to create my own paper stickers I find that I really enjoy getting waterproof, durable vinyl stickers made, instead. My personal laptops are covered with these types of vinyl stickers and I am thrilled to pieces when someone sends me a photo of their laptop with one of my stickers stuck on it.
